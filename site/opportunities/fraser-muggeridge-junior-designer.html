About Fraser Muggeridge Studio

Fraser Muggeridge Studio is a London-based graphic design practice led by Fraser Muggeridge. The studio has a strong specialism in typography, editorial design, and visual identity for arts and cultural organisations. Their client list reads like a map of the UK's serious cultural institutions — they produce books, catalogues, identities, and visual systems for galleries, museums, and arts publishers.

The studio's work is characterised by typographic rigour and intellectual depth. They are not a commercial branding studio producing generic corporate identities — they work at the edge of graphic design culture, where design and art thinking overlap.
