About Bompas & Parr

Bompas & Parr is a London-based experiential design and creative studio founded by Sam Bompas and Harry Parr. They are internationally known for pioneering edible art and immersive environments — from architectural jelly structures and multisensory dining events to major museum installations and brand experiences for some of the world's most prestigious cultural institutions and companies.

Their studio is based at the Barbican, placing them in the heart of London's arts infrastructure. Past projects span Tate Modern, the V&A, major food festivals, luxury brand activations, and commissioned public art. The studio is known for intellectual rigour and visual wit — making it a serious, ambitious environment for an intern or junior hire.
