About Luke Halls Studio

Luke Halls is one of the UK's leading projection and moving image designers for live performance. The studio creates immersive video design for theatre, opera, dance, and live events — working with major venues and touring productions globally. Past collaborators include some of the most prominent names in contemporary performance.

The studio describes itself as "multidisciplinary" — the work spans graphic design, illustration, animation, and cinematographic image-making, all applied to the specific demands of live performance: timing, scale, narrative, and the relationship with physical space.
