# Maia Quality Audit

Started: 2026-05-02

Purpose: correct over-promoted / under-researched opportunity pages. Standard is evidence first: verified source links, active role status where possible, and explicit separation between company credibility and job-post verification.

## Evidence standard

For every opportunity, classify separately:
1. **Organisation verified?** Does the organisation/site clearly exist?
2. **Role verified?** Is the role visible on an official/source page, not only inferred from a screenshot?
3. **Active/current?** Are deadline/status/timing still valid?
4. **Fit for Maia?** Is it aligned with Maia's creative/exhibition/design-development lane?
5. **Action status:** application-ready, research lead, reference only, low-priority, or remove/demote.
